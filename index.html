The Program

N’de Nibimwidoon is a holistic seven day land based program developed to lessen sexual assault for Anishinaabe-kwek.

  • Traditional Teachings
  • Ceremony
  • Sharing Circles
  • Art Based Self-Explorations
  • Sexual Assault Resistance Training
  • Building Self-Empowerment Through Sexual Health Education
  • Self Defence Training
  • Designed for Youth 15-25
  • Small Groups(maximum: 15)

Woven together like a sweetgrass braid, the components of this program fosters self-awareness, self-determination and resilience.

N’de Nibimwidoon - I Carry My Heart was designed by Michelle Hrynyk under the supervision of consultants Marie McGregor-Pitawanakwat and Mark Seabrook

For more information on the background and development of this program check out Michelle's thesis: Towards the development of a culturally sensitive, empowerment-based sexual assault resistance model for Anishinaabe women
